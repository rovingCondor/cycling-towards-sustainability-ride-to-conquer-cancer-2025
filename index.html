Ride to Conquer Cancer

This year marks our first time participating in the Ride to Conquer Cancer as part of Liftow's Cycling for Sustainability Campaign and our ongoing corporate social responsibility initiatives. We made the shift from the Bike for Brain Health to the Ride to Conquer Cancer due to the flexibility it offers, allowing us to organize our own team-building ride while supporting a meaningful cause.

Ways to Participate:

Serious Fundraisers / Riders
(June 7-8)

Raise $2,000 to $2500 qualifies you to ride 100km or 200km ride to Hamilton - Niagara on The Lake.

Casual Fundraisers / Riders
(Ride Your Way 2025)

Ride as far as you want wherever you want. No minimum funds raised but you can get a cycling jersey if you raise $1,500.

Liftow Team-Building Event
(Date TBD)

Join us for our own "Ride Your Way" event, designed to bring the team together while making a difference.
